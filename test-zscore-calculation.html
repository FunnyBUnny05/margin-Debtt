WHAT IS Z-SCORE?
Z-Score measures how many standard deviations a sector's relative performance is from its historical average.

Negative Z-Score (< -2): Sector is UNDERPERFORMING vs benchmark ‚Üí "CHEAP"
Positive Z-Score (> +2): Sector is OUTPERFORMING vs benchmark ‚Üí "EXTENDED/EXPENSIVE"

This is RELATIVE performance, not absolute valuation!
